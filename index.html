About Me

Hello my name is Marcello Miranda and I like to create beautiful, usable, and professional websites. All my websites are lovingly hand coded using best practice accessibility and the latest W3C web standards guidelines, resulting in semantic and SEO friendly HTML and CSS.

I bring 10 years of experience and a variety of skills to any business environment. My primary mission is to help companies overhaul their websites, replacing them with high quality web design using the most current technology available. For creating my websites weapons of choice are HTML 5, CSS 3, PHP, Wordpress and Photoshop. Im always working on my skill sets to help improve my quality of work.
